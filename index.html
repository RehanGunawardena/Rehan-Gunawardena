RESEARCH

Research that links mathematical theory with chemical and scientific questions.

01

Topological Indices & Drug Design

Developing and evaluating molecular descriptors for pharmaceutical compounds, including anti-tuberculosis drugs, dendrimers, and other drug-related molecular structures.

  • Revan and Rehan–Lanel indices
  • QSPR-oriented molecular analysis
  • Polynomial approaches to graph descriptors

02

Graph Theory & Mathematical Structures

Investigating graph invariants, knot types, graph polynomials, nanotubes, nanotorus structures, and the mathematical properties underlying complex networks.

  • Degree- and distance-based indices
  • Knot theory and transforming metrics
  • Graph polynomials and irregularity measures

03

Applied Statistics & Modelling

Applying statistical designs, regression, multivariate methods, sampling techniques, and data science tools to research questions in science and society.

  • Experimental design
  • Statistical computing
  • Data analysis and scientific communication
